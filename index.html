Weight Training

Weight training involves using resistance, such as free weights or machines, to build muscle and improve strength. It boosts metabolism, enhances bone density, and promotes overall fitness. Incorporating proper technique and progressive overload is essential for optimal results, making it beneficial for both beginners and experienced lifters alike

General Fitness

General fitness encompasses a balanced approach to physical health, including cardiovascular endurance, strength training, flexibility, and nutrition. Regular exercise—like walking, running, or yoga—improves overall well-being, boosts energy levels, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle contributes to mental and physical resilience, enhancing quality of life.

Functional Training

Functional training emphasizes exercises that mimic daily activities, improving strength, stability, and flexibility. It includes bodyweight movements, resistance bands, and free weights, focusing on core strength and coordination. This approach enhances performance in everyday tasks and sports while reducing injury risk, making it suitable for all fitness levels.
